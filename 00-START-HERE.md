## ðŸ’¡ Pro Tips

### Content Writing
- **Be specific**: Use numbers and metrics
- **Show impact**: What changed because of your work?
- **Tell stories**: Make projects relatable
- **Be authentic**: Let your personality show

### Images
- **Headshot**: Professional but approachable
- **Projects**: Screenshots of actual interfaces work best
- **Quality over quantity**: 3 great projects > 10 mediocre ones

### Customization
- **Start simple**: Use defaults first, customize later
- **Test changes**: Always check in browser
- **Commit often**: Save your work with Git
- **Iterate**: Your first version doesn't have to be perfect

### Deployment
- **Vercel is easiest**: One-click deploy, automatic HTTPS
- **Custom domain**: Makes you look more professional
- **Test before sharing**: Preview on multiple devices
